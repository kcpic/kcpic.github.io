25 Essential Destinations

1. Osaka Castle Park (Nishinomaru Garden)

With over 3,000 cherry trees, this is Osaka's premier Sakura spot. The Nishinomaru Garden offers a stunning view of the castle tower framed by blossoms.

2. The Philosopher's Path (Tetsugaku-no-michi)

A stone path through Kyoto's Higashiyama district. It follows a canal lined by hundreds of cherry trees.

3. Kamo River (Kamogawa)

Perfect for a picnic. Walk south from Demachiyanagi for a "Sakura tunnel" effect.

4. Fushimi Inari Taisha Osaka-Kyoto

Famous for vermilion torii gates. Stunning early spring greenery contrast.

5. Kiyomizu-dera Temple

UNESCO site with iconic wooden stage. Magical night illumination during Sakura season.

6. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove & Togetsukyo Bridge

Walk the bamboo paths then head to the bridge for mountain-side cherry blossoms.

10. Dotonbori (Osaka Foodie Street)

The neon heart of Osaka. Perfect for street food hopping after a day of Sakura viewing.

24. Nijo Castle (Nijo-jo)

Features a variety of cherry tree species, ensuring blooms throughout your entire 10-day trip.

25. Japan Mint (Sakura-no-toorinuke)

Essential for your dates! The Mint opens its gates for one week in April for rare, late-blooming double-petal varieties (Yaezakura).
