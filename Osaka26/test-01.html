Osaka Highlights

0 Kansai International Airport

Entrance Port to Osaka, just outside the city

1 Osaka Castle Park

The massive park surrounding the castle tower comes alive with over 3,000 cherry trees. The Nishinomaru Garden is the primary viewing spot for evening light-ups.

2 Japan Mint (Sakura Passage)

Known for rare, late-blooming double-petal cherry trees. This passage is only open for one week in mid-April, perfect for the end of your trip.

3 Dotonbori

While not a primary sakura spot, it's the cultural heart of Osaka's food scene. Visit after a day of viewing for Takoyaki and Okonomiyaki.

Kyoto Highlights

4 The Philosopher's Path

A stone path following a canal lined with hundreds of cherry trees, connecting Ginkaku-ji to the Nanzen-ji area.

5 Kiyomizu-dera Temple

An iconic Buddhist temple with a massive wooden stage that offers breathtaking views of the city framed by spring blossoms.

6 Arashiyama & Togetsukyo Bridge

The forested mountainside turns pink in late March. Walking across the bridge is a quintessential Kyoto spring experience.

7 Keage Incline

A former railway track that is now a walking path lined with cherry trees. One of the most unique photo spots in Kyoto.

Others

8 Nara Park

Located an hour from both cities, Nara Park offers a unique mix of ancient temples, free-roaming deer, and massive cherry blossom trees.
